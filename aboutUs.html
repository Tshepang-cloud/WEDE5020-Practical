About Golden Crumbs

Golden Crumbs is a proudly South African business based in the heart of Cape Town, dedicated to creating artisanal biscuits and traditional sweets that bring back the warmth of home-baked goodness.

Our bakery started with a simple mission: to celebrate local flavors and timeless recipes while delivering consistent quality to our community. Every product is handcrafted with love, using locally sourced ingredients and a whole lot of passion.

At Golden Crumbs, we believe that food is more than just a treat — it's an experience. Whether you're enjoying our crunchy rusks with your morning coffee, gifting a custom biscuit box to someone special, or discovering our sugar-free range, you're tasting our commitment to quality and care.

Our true intention is to make every customer feel valued and satisfied. We strive to create a welcoming experience, both in-store and online, where you can explore, taste, and connect with our brand. From everyday indulgences to special celebrations, we’re here to make every crumb count.
